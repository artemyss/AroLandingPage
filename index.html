Aro
Adventure is a click away

Aro is for the wild-hearted who walk their own path. Bring out your inner explorer, choose a destination and let Aro point you in the right direction -- the journey is up to you! Not only for adventure, it's practical too! When you are in a crowded parking lot, drop a pin before you leave your car and Aro will guide you back later. Out hiking and you find a great spot? Drop a pin to make your way back with more friends.

Using Aro

To use Aro, you need to create an <Ionic account. Once you have your Ionic account set up, follow the instructions in the gitHub ReadMe to download the repo and deploy with Ionic. Check out the demo video for a preview of the app and an intro to using Aro on your iPhone.
